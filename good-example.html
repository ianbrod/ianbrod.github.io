Market Overview

North American Market Scope

Market Growth Drivers

  • • Competitive job market dynamics
  • • Increasing ATS complexity
  • • Remote work expansion
  • • International professional demand

The North American market for professional resume writing services is substantial and experiencing steady growth, projected to expand from $2.5 billion in 2024 to $4.1 billion by 2033, representing a 6.5% CAGR [128]. The U.S. holds the largest segment, accounting for 38% of the global market share in 2023 [154].
